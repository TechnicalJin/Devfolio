Who I Am

I build systems that scale, APIs that make sense, and applications that solve real problems.

I'm a full-stack engineer with 2 years of experience turning complex requirements into clean, working solutions. My focus is backend architecture—Spring Boot, MySQL, REST APIs—but I handle the full stack when needed.
